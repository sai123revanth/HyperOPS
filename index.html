The Problem: Hidden Impact

Consumers want to be sustainable, but financial transactions are disconnected from environmental impact. We lack visibility into the carbon cost of our daily spending.

The Solution: Conscious Capital

ECOPAY transforms transaction data into actionable eco-insights. We empower users to track, reduce, and offset their footprint with every swipe.

Core Features

  • Real-Time Carbon Calc: Instant CO₂ estimates for every purchase based on merchant category.
  • Green Marketplace: Seamless integration with verified carbon offset projects.
  • Eco-Scoring: Gamified sustainability metrics to drive behavioral change.
