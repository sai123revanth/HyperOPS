GREENIQ

Automated Carbon Footprint Tracking via Transaction Analytics

Data Analytics API Integration ESG

Context & Motivation

Consumers increasingly seek to make environmentally responsible choices but lack visibility into the carbon impact of their everyday financial transactions. The gap between intent and action is often a lack of data.

System Objective

Design a green finance analytics platform that converts transaction histories into personalized carbon footprint insights, enabling informed and sustainable spending behavior.

Core Functional Components

  • Merchant Categorization Engine Maps vendors to standardized carbon emission factors.
  • Impact Dashboards Visualize CO2 output across spending (Travel, Food, Retail).
  • Offset Marketplace Direct integration to compensate footprint via verified projects.

Design Considerations

Accuracy of emission datasets and strict privacy controls over financial data are essential. Transparency and trust are critical for user adoption.
