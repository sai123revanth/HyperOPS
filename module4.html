Scope 3 Transparency

Core Function

Decodes the environmental DNA of every transaction. We cross-reference spending data with global ESG databases to expose the true cost of commerce, detect greenwashing, and highlight verified sustainable merchants.

Key Metrics

  • ESG Radar: Real-time Environmental, Social, and Governance scoring (0-100).
  • Risk Flags: Instant alerts for labor violations, plastic waste, or fossil fuel intensity.
  • Greener Swaps: AI-driven alternative recommendations (e.g., "Switch to Local Farmers Market").

Innovation Layer

  • Supply Chain Mapping: Geo-spatial visualization of product origins and logistics carbon cost.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Aggregates community trust scores to verify corporate claims vs. reality.
  • Impact Simulation: Forecasts portfolio score improvement by shifting 20% of spend to Grade A merchants.
